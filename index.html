I’ve been building and developing the future in one place my entire UX career. From day one, I’ve poured my heart, soul, and a ton of wireframes into making things better, always striving to push the boundaries of what’s possible. It’s been a journey of growth, challenges, and more “Aha!” moments than I can count, and I wouldn’t trade it for anything.
When the Design and Product teams were freshly hatched, I had the honor (and a bit of chaos) of being the first designer on board. Think of me as the first squirrel to venture out onto a busy road—I took the leap and grew the design process and built the design system, component library, and UX Research process from scratch! While I truly appreciated the feedback and insights from my Product team, Engineers, and Stakeholders, the responsibility of creating a UX-focused atmosphere was all part of my job description. It was a wild ride that sharpened my design skills and solidified my passion for crafting user-centric solutions.
